3D Talking Avatar

Welcome to the 3D Talking Avatar

This web application allows you to interact with a 3D avatar powered by text-to-speech technology. Select a model, type your message, and watch the avatar speak your text in real-time. You can also record the avatar's animation and save interaction logs for later review.

Button Functions

  • ChatGPT Mode: Toggles between direct speech mode and a ChatGPT-like conversational mode. In direct mode, the avatar speaks exactly what you type. In ChatGPT mode, the app processes your input for more natural responses.
  • Record Video: Toggles video recording of the avatar. When active, it records the avatar's animation and audio, saving it as a downloadable video file (e.g., avatar_[timestamp].webm).
  • Save Log: Downloads a log of your chat interactions, useful for debugging and reviewing past conversations with the avatar.
